Marketplace Pricing

Your workspace to turn ideas into action

Kill dead knowledge — the gap between ideas and action — and turn what you know into real results. Organize your thoughts, channel your creativity, and bridge the space between understanding and execution.

Built on 7 Pillars

Core Workspace

Your command center for everything that matters. Pulsar organizes tasks, projects, and ideas into a single intuitive interface, reducing friction and clutter. It adapts to the way you work, giving you focus without forcing structure.

Knowledge & Learning

Capture, connect, and evolve your knowledge effortlessly. Notes, references, and insights are linked contextually, making it easy to retrieve and build on them over time. Pulsar helps transform raw information into actionable understanding, so learning becomes continuous and intuitive.

Productivity

Turn ideas into action without losing momentum. Smart task management, goal tracking, and workflow automation reduce repetitive work and mental overhead. Focus stays on what matters, while Pulsar handles the organizational heavy lifting.

Customization

Pulsar molds to your workflow, not the other way around. Themes, layouts, and modular tools let you create a workspace that feels truly yours. Every feature can be tuned to match your habits, priorities, and style.

Collaboration

Seamless teamwork without the chaos. Real-time editing, shared spaces, and contextual discussion layers keep everyone aligned. Collaboration in Pulsar is natural, effortless, and productive, whether working in small teams or large organizations.

Data & Insights

Your activity becomes actionable intelligence. Pulsar tracks patterns, generates analytics, and surfaces trends that help you make informed decisions. Insightful visualizations transform everyday work into meaningful guidance.

Integration & Expansion

Connect with the tools you already use and expand beyond them. APIs, plugins, and intelligent connectors bring your ecosystem together, while Pulsar grows as your needs evolve. Integration ensures nothing is lost, and expansion ensures nothing is out of reach.

Smart Focus

Automatically highlights your most important tasks or next best move.

Idea Fusion

Connect scattered thoughts into structured action plans. Pulsar links your ideas like constellations to form a clear path forward.

Custom Dashboard

Build your workspace your way. Pulsar's dashboard is a fully customizable canvas where you can arrange widgets, charts, and tools exactly how you want them.

AI-Powered Insights

Pulsar learns your habits and suggests faster, smarter workflows for improved productivity.

Adaptive System

Enjoy smooth transitions and a workspace that adapts to how you work.

Accelerators

Sets of curated tools, templates, and projects to help you learn and practice efficiently. They guide you through exercises and real-world applications, making mastery faster and easier.

AI That Grows With You

Invisible at first, indispensable over time. Your AI evolves through progressive tiers.

Atom

Background Organizer

AI quietly manages notes and highlights key points so you can work frictionlessly.

  • Organizes workspace and notes automatically
  • Highlights important points without interrupting workflow
  • Provides subtle support in the background

Molecule

Guided Helper

AI starts suggesting actions and summarizing notes for a smoother workflow.

  • Summarizes notes and highlights key actions
  • Offers reminders and prioritization cues
  • Supports multiple projects or note types

Neuron

Insightful Collaborator

AI connects ideas, offers insights, and helps manage more complex tasks.

  • Provides deep insights and actionable recommendations
  • Connects related ideas for better understanding
  • Supports team collaboration and advanced workflows

Quantum

Personal AI Assistant

Full AI assistant for tasks, learning, insights, and automation.

  • Acts as a personal AI assistant across tasks and projects
  • Predicts outcomes and automates workflows
  • Integrates seamlessly with other tools
  • Adapts to your habits and preferences
